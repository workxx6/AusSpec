OUR HISTORY

1993

COMMERCIALISATION OF PIMA

Commercialisation of PIMA (portable infrared mineral analyser, the first truly field portable spectrometer) in Australasia (early days for AusSpec and worked under the name of Spic)

1990

AUSSPEC FIELD TEST

AusSpec staff involved in field testing of first prototype field spectrometer, the PIMA, developed by Integrated Spectronics

1990

DEVELOPMENT OF THE TSG

Development of the TSG (The Spectral Geologist) software. An AusSpec consortium funded project to develop a user-friendly commercial spectral processing package, AusSpec designed an interface to front an early piece of spectral processing software (called XSpectra), which became “TSG”. Since then AusSpec has worked closely with CSIRO developing and beta testing variants of TSG (Viewer, Lite and Core) and has provided CSIRO with a business platform to market and sell TSG worldwide. This platform provides both TSG clients and agents with sales, technical support, specialist training, on-line webinars and video tutorials.
